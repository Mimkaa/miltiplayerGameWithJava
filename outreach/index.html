Multiplayer Twist

This is not your typical co-op game. In Think Outside The Room, each character is controlled by two players – one handles movement, the other handles actions like jumping or grabbing.

It’s a true test of communication, coordination, and patience. To succeed, you must think as one. Laugh together. Fail together. Win together.
